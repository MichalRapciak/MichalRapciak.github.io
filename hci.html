ACCESSIBILITY & USABILITY EVALUATION OF GOOGLE MAPS

BY MICHAL RAPCIAK, MARCH 2022

GOOGLE MAPS - https://www.google.com/maps

INTRODUCTION

Google Maps is one of the most used websites by many.
Everyone knows it, everyone holds it up as the benchmark for navigation apps.
But why is that so? What makes Google Maps so good?
Is there anything that can be improved in it? That is what this report will be about.
I will be studying Google Maps - it's usability and accessibility, it's upsides and downsides, and why it is as popular as it is.
It's not very often we have a website that becomes such a standout benchmark for such a long time.
I want to find out what makes users return to Google Maps - what does it have that other map websites do not?
How accessible is it to users? There is many different uses for maps, so how does Google Maps live up to that?
That is what I will be exploring in this report.
