AUBREY GRAHAM

ABOUT DRAKE

Aubrey Drake Graham, also known by his artist pseudonym "Drake", is a rapper, singer and actor from Toronto, Canada.
He is best known for his many best-selling albums such as 'Take Care', 'Views', 'Scorpion' and 'Certified Lover Boy'.
In his long-running music career he reached the 'Billboard 200' Number 1 spot a total of 10 times so far, and has collected 9 hits on the 'Billboard 100'.
He is credited with pushing the genre of Rap and Hip-Hop towards mainstream glory, merging it with Pop and making it the biggest music genre in the world.
Outside of music, he has starred as an actor in TV shows such as 'Degrassi', and movies such as the 2012 cult classic 'Ice Age: Continental Drift'.
He is also the owner of a very popular, critically acclaimed and successful lifestyle fashion brand 'October's Very Own', simply known as 'OVO'.
OVO is called October's Very Own as Drake was born in October, and stated that he is a very nocturnal person.

Top 10 Album Ranking

Drake had many great albums that topped the worldwide charts, so it is difficult to rank them without disrespecting some of them.
But as a fan page this is a necessary part of the process, so unfortunately some sacrifices will have to be made as an album will have to be bottom :(.
This is my ranking of the Top 10 Drake albums as of 2022.

  • 1. Certified Lover Boy
  • 2. Scorpion
  • 3. So Far Gone
  • 4. More Life
  • 5. Take Care
  • 6. Thank Me Later
  • 7. Nothing Was The Same
  • 8. Views
  • 9. Scary Hours 2
  • 10. If You're Reading This It's Too Late

Certified Lover Boy just has to take the top spot for me. It is the best project Drake has released, simply said. It's Drake at his best.
Songs like 'Way Too Sexy' and 'No Friends In The Industry' are some of the best radio singles Drake has released, and the entire album is very innovative.
Scorpion is a close second as it is filled to the brim with great songs from Drake, including 'God's Plan', 'Nice For What' and 'Nonstop'.
So Far Gone is an earlier, much more raw and hungry Drake as he tried to break out onto the scene, successfully so.
More Life and Take Care are both great albums, filled with slower and sadder songs.
Thank Me Later and Nothing Was The Same are both good projects, although the low points can drag on for too long.
While Views and Scary Hours are 2, they are mostly forgettable outside of a few amazing tracks.
While at Number 10, IYRTITL is still a good piece of music and should be treated with respect, as it was Drake leaving his comfort zone for something new.

Overall, while CLB and Scorpion are at the top, all 10 albums are great and worth a listen to.
