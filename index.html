Broadway Avenue

Broadway avenue is a sunny and beautiful part of Detroit filled with high rise buildings and interesting architecture.

Book Tower

Book tower is a beautiful tower located in the middle of Detroit. It's stood strong since the 1920s and is a massive landmark for the city.

Belmont

Belmont is a scenic area of Detroit. As opposed to the busy city center near Broadway Avenue, Belmont is full of small but beautiful houses.
