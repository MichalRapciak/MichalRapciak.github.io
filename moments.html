MY FAVOURITE DRAKE MOMENTS

Drake has had a long career filled with funny and interesting moments, so this list is not difficult to make.
If you're in the industry for as long as Drake there is ought to be some funny things happening!
This is a short list of my 3 favourite Drake moments.

NUMBER 1 - HOT SAUCE INCIDENT

One of the first things to come to mind is the recent 'Hot Sauce' incident that Drake was under fire for.
It was revealed by tabloid media that Drake allegedly filled a condom with hot sauce.
This was meant to be an ingenious way of getting rid of the sperm in the condom,
making sure that the model he was having intercourse with could not impregnate herself without his consent.
